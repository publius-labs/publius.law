The Name

Why Publius?

The name Publius comes from the pseudonym used by Alexander Hamilton, James Madison, and John Jay when writing The Federalist Papers — a series of essays that helped shape American democracy.

Like those foundational writings, our platform is designed to strengthen the pillars of justice by making sophisticated legal tools available to all practitioners, not just the privileged few.

We believe that access to powerful legal technology shouldn't be determined by the size of your firm. Every attorney deserves the tools to serve their clients effectively — and that's what Publius delivers.
