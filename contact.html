Frequently Asked Questions

What types of law firms does Publius serve?

Publius is designed specifically for small to medium-sized law firms and solo practitioners. Our platform scales to meet the needs of firms of all sizes, from individual attorneys to practices with 100+ attorneys.

How secure is my client data?

We take security extremely seriously. Publius offers both SOC 2 compliant cloud hosting and on-premise deployment options. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we maintain strict access controls and audit logs.

How long does implementation take?

Implementation timelines vary based on your firm's size and needs. Most firms are up and running within 2-4 weeks. We provide dedicated onboarding support to ensure a smooth transition.

Do you offer training and support?

Yes! We provide comprehensive training during onboarding and ongoing support thereafter. Our support team is available via email to assist with any questions or issues.
