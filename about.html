About Publius

Empowering small law firms with enterprise-grade technology

Our Mission

We believe small law firms and solo attorneys deserve the same firepower as the largest firms — without the price tag that keeps you locked out.

Publius was built to level the playing field. Our AI-powered legal technology platform accelerates research, discovery, and trial preparation, cutting days of work into hours at a fraction of the traditional cost.

We're not here to replace attorneys. We're here to supercharge them, giving smaller practices the tools they need to compete, win, and thrive in an increasingly complex legal landscape.

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do at Publius

Access to Justice

Legal technology should be accessible to all attorneys, regardless of firm size. We're committed to democratizing access to powerful legal tools.

Security & Trust

Your clients' data is sacred. We provide SOC 2 compliant cloud hosting and on-premise deployment options to ensure maximum security and control.

Continuous Innovation

The legal landscape is constantly evolving. We're committed to staying at the forefront of legal technology, continuously improving our platform.

Why Publius?

The name "Publius" comes from the pseudonym used by Alexander Hamilton, James Madison, and John Jay when writing The Federalist Papers — a series of essays that helped shape American democracy.

Like those foundational writings, our platform is designed to strengthen the pillars of justice by making sophisticated legal tools available to all practitioners, not just the privileged few.

We believe that access to powerful legal technology shouldn't be determined by the size of your firm. Every attorney deserves the tools to serve their clients effectively, and that's what Publius delivers.
