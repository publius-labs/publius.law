About us

Publius is developed by a team with long-standing experience designing and delivering enterprise software systems for document-intensive and regulated environments. Prior to launching Publius, members of the core engineering and leadership team worked through Ideom, a software services firm with more than twenty years of experience supporting complex data workflows across legal, media, and enterprise contexts. ​

The company is currently in an early development phase, engaging with prospective users to refine the platform and prepare for broader availability.
