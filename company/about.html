About us

Publius is developed by a team with long-standing experience designing and delivering enterprise software systems for document-intensive and regulated environments. Prior to launching Publius, members of the core engineering and leadership team worked through Ideom LLC, a software services firm with more than fifteen years of experience supporting complex data workflows across legal, media, and enterprise contexts. ​

That background includes building and maintaining large-scale document management and workflow systems where reliability, accuracy, and operational discipline are essential. This experience informs Publius's emphasis on stability, transparency, and responsible system design. ​

Publius is based in central Pennsylvania, Harrisburg, and operates with a distributed team of engineers, product specialists, and advisors. The leadership team includes experienced professionals in software engineering, product development, and business operations, supported by contributors with backgrounds in AI/ML systems, full-stack development, and legal technology. ​

The company is currently in an early commercialization phase, engaging with prospective users to refine the platform and prepare for broader availability.
